About Us



Welcome to Audrey's Cookie Haven, where flour flies, chocolate chips reign supreme, and every cookie is a bite of bliss! Founded in the cozy, imaginative kitchen of Audrey herself, our little haven began with a simple mission: to make people smile with cookies so delightful, you'd think they were a figment of your imagination. But no, they’re real, and they’re spectacular! Audrey, a self-taught baker, cookie connoisseur, and sprinkle enthusiast, decided it was time to spread her wings (and maybe a little flour) beyond her kitchen after her creations began causing neighborhood traffic jams and mild cookie frenzies. And so, Audrey's Cookie Haven was born—a place dedicated to anyone who believes that the right cookie can turn a bad day around or make a good day even better.


Our Cookies

Here at Audrey's Cookie Haven, we take cookies seriously—well, as seriously as one can while wearing polka-dotted aprons and dancing to 80s hits on repeat. Each cookie is crafted with the finest, locally-sourced ingredients and an extra scoop of joy, making them perfect for dessert, a snack, or even breakfast (we won’t judge). From the timeless Chocolate Chip Calamity—where we challenge the laws of physics by how many chocolate chips we can cram into one cookie—to the audacious Snickerdoodle Doo, our cookies are here to bring a little whimsy and a lot of flavor to your day.


Why Choose Us?

Besides mastering the sacred art of cookie-making, we at Audrey's Cookie Haven also believe in magic—cookie magic, that is. It’s that special something you feel with every bite, a taste that transports you back to your grandma's kitchen or that one unforgettable afternoon baking (and eating) cookies with friends.


Our Promise

We promise to always bake with heart, serve with a smile, and sprinkle a little fun wherever we go. Whether you need a box for a special occasion or a single cookie to brighten your day, we've got you covered. So come on over to Audrey’s Cookie Haven. Our doors are always open (unless we’re chasing an escaped cookie—yes, they occasionally sprout legs and make a run for it). Share a laugh, take a cookie (or twelve), and savor the sweet taste of homemade happiness.
